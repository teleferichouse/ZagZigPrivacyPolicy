Privacy Policy for ZigZag Game
Last Updated: November 16, 2025

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you play our mobile game "ZigZag" (the "Game"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect
2.1 Information Collected Automatically
Game Progress Data: High scores, current level progress, in-game currency (gems), and game statistics
Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
Usage Information: Game session duration, feature usage, and crash reports
2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect
Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
Location Data: We do not access or collect your precise location
Contact Information: We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
3. How We Use Information
3.1 Game Functionality
Save your game progress, settings, and achievements locally on your device
Provide in-game rewards and currency management
Maintain high scores and statistics
3.2 Advertising
Display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
Measure ad performance and effectiveness
Provide rewarded video advertisements for in-game benefits
3.3 Game Improvement
Analyze gameplay patterns to improve game balance and user experience
Identify and fix technical issues and bugs
Optimize game performance
4. Data Storage
4.1 Local Storage
All game progress data is stored locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system
This includes: high scores, gem count, purchased items, and game settings
Data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
4.2 Third-Party Services
We integrate with the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob

Collects advertising identifier and device information for ad serving
May use cookies and similar technologies for personalized advertising
Governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
